# Progress Tracker

This tracker is for Week 1 interview preparation.

Use it to record what you studied, what you understood, what remains weak, and
what needs follow-up before moving to Week 2.

The goal is not to look busy. The goal is to make weak areas visible.

## Week 1 focus

Week 1 covers:

- LLM fundamentals.
- Latest NVIDIA platforms.
- The LLM/GPU systems bridge.
- Behavioral strategy.
- A Week 1 diagnostic quiz.

## How to use this tracker

Use this file at three points:

1. Before studying, record your baseline confidence.
2. After each module, record weak areas and follow-up actions.
3. After the quiz, record scores and remediation work.

Do not write long essays here. Keep entries short and actionable.
